# TIHC Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

TIHC is a browser extension for TiDB troubleshooting assistance.

## What we collect

- User input you type in the extension chat.
- Assistant responses returned from the configured backend API.

## How data is used

- To process your request and generate troubleshooting answers.
- To improve reliability and debugging of the service.

## What we do not collect

- No Google account login data from the extension.
- No payment information.
- No unrelated browsing history collection.

## Data sharing

- Chat content is sent to the backend endpoint configured by the extension for response generation.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Data retention

- Message retention depends on backend service configuration and operational logs.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@askaric.com
